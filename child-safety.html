Romo Child Safety Policy

Last Updated: July 23, 2025

18+ ADULTS ONLY

Romo is exclusively designed for adults aged 18 and older. Minors are strictly prohibited from using this service under any circumstances.

Our Commitment to Child Safety

At Romo, we take child safety extremely seriously. As an adult-oriented dating platform featuring live video calls and intimate messaging, we have implemented comprehensive measures to ensure that minors cannot access our services and that our platform remains exclusively for consenting adults.

This Child Safety Policy outlines our strict age verification processes, safety measures, and compliance with international child protection standards. We have zero tolerance for any content or behavior that could endanger minors.

Age Verification and Restrictions

Strict 18+ Policy

  • Minimum Age Requirement: All users must be at least 18 years old to create an account or use any Romo services
  • Age Declaration: Users must confirm their age during registration and acknowledge that providing false information is prohibited
  • Photo Verification: Our mandatory photo verification system helps detect potentially underage users
  • Device Restrictions: The app includes technical restrictions to prevent installation on devices registered to minors
  • Parental Controls: We support and encourage parents to use device-level parental controls to restrict access

Account Creation Safeguards

  • Multiple age confirmation checkpoints during onboarding
  • Terms of Service explicitly state 18+ requirement
  • Email verification required before account activation
  • Profile review process to identify suspicious accounts
  • AI-powered detection of potentially underage photos

Content Moderation and Safety Measures

Proactive Content Monitoring

  • AI Content Scanning: All uploaded photos undergo automated analysis to detect potentially underage individuals
  • Human Review: Flagged content is reviewed by trained moderators within 24 hours
  • User Reporting: Easy-to-use reporting system for users to flag suspicious accounts or content
  • Video Call Monitoring: Automated systems detect and flag inappropriate behavior during video calls
  • Message Filtering: Advanced algorithms scan messages for predatory language or attempts to solicit minors

Immediate Response Protocols

  • Suspected underage accounts are immediately suspended pending investigation
  • Zero-tolerance policy for users attempting to contact minors
  • Automatic reporting to appropriate authorities when required by law
  • Permanent bans for any child safety violations
  • IP and device blocking to prevent re-registration

Technical Safety Implementation

Platform Security

  • App Store Ratings: Romo is rated 17+ on iOS App Store and Mature 17+ on Google Play Store
  • Geolocation Restrictions: The app respects regional age of consent laws and may restrict access in certain jurisdictions
  • Data Protection: All user data is encrypted and stored securely to prevent unauthorized access
  • Regular Security Audits: Third-party security assessments ensure our safety measures remain effective
  • Staff Training: All employees receive mandatory training on child safety protocols and legal requirements

User Education

  • Clear safety guidelines provided during onboarding
  • Regular safety reminders and tips within the app
  • Educational content about online dating safety
  • Resources for reporting inappropriate behavior

Legal Compliance and Cooperation

Romo fully complies with all applicable laws regarding child safety, including:

  • COPPA Compliance: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • GDPR Article 8: We comply with EU regulations regarding processing of children's data
  • International Standards: Our policies align with global best practices for child protection online
  • Law Enforcement Cooperation: We maintain direct channels with law enforcement agencies
  • NCMEC Reporting: We report suspected child exploitation to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children when required

Transparency Reports

We publish annual transparency reports detailing our child safety efforts, including the number of accounts removed for age violations and our cooperation with law enforcement agencies.

Reporting and Enforcement

How to Report Concerns

If you suspect a user is under 18 or encounter any child safety concerns:

  • Use the in-app reporting feature immediately
  • Email our safety team: lakliech.devs@proton.me
  • For emergencies, contact local law enforcement first

Our Response Commitment

  • 24/7 Monitoring: Our safety team monitors reports around the clock
  • Rapid Response: Child safety reports are prioritized and addressed within 2 hours
  • Follow-up: We provide updates to reporters when appropriate
  • Continuous Improvement: We regularly update our safety measures based on new threats and feedback

Parental Guidance

For Parents and Guardians

While Romo is designed exclusively for adults, we recognize that parents may have concerns about their teens accessing dating apps. We recommend:

  • Device Controls: Use built-in parental controls on iOS and Android devices
  • App Store Restrictions: Enable age-appropriate app download restrictions
  • Open Communication: Discuss online safety and appropriate relationship boundaries with your teens
  • Regular Monitoring: Check your teen's device for inappropriate apps or content
  • Educational Resources: Utilize online safety resources from organizations like Common Sense Media

Important Note:

If you discover your minor child has created a Romo account, please contact us immediately at lakliech.devs@proton.me for immediate account termination.
